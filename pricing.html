Session Fees

The initial assessment session is free. After that the cost will be £40 for a 50 minute session.

Getting Started

I will offer you a free assessment session where we will determine whether I am the right counsellor for you and whether you feel happy to begin working with me.

During the first session we will explore what has brought you to therapy and how we can work together to help you.
