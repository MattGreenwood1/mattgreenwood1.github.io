About Matt

My name is Matt Greenwood and I offer a counselling service in various locations throughout County Durham and Newcastle.

I am a registered member of the British Association for Counselling and Psychotherapy (BACP). Membership of this organisation guarantees a high standard of competence, ethics and professionalism.

If you are suffering from difficulties that you feel you need support with, I can offer you a safe space to talk openly, confidentially and without judgement. Together we can agree a way to make positive and lasting change.

Location

My main location for counselling is in the centre of Durham, within easy reach of Bus and Train stations and with free parking available.
