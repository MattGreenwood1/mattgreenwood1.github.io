About Matt

I am a registered member of the British Association for Counselling and Psychotherapy (BACP). The Professional Standards Authority for Health and Social Care has set a quality mark of accreditation for this association.

I carry out counselling for the NHS in various locations throughout the North East region.

If you are suffering from difficulties that you feel you need support with, I provide you a safe space to talk openly, confidentially and without judgement. Together we can agree a way to make positive and lasting change.
